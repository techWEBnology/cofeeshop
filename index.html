Community nights

More Than a Café: Your Evening Hangout

Weekly events curated for people who love stories, music and calm corners in the middle of the city.

Weekly schedule
  • Wednesday – Open Mic: Poetry, stand‑up, storytelling. Slots announced on Instagram every Monday.
  • Friday – Indie Gig: Live acoustic sets from upcoming artists, no cover charge.
  • Sunday – Board Game Social: Join strangers for strategy, co‑op and party games.

We keep volumes conversation‑friendly. Working guests are welcome outside live performance windows, and there are always laptop‑free zones for those who just want to unplug.
